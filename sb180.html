THE 180° SPIN

Ollies with a twist!

How to execute:

  1. Prepare to Ollie as usual.
  2. Swing your shoulders in the direction you want spin as you Ollie.
  3. Scoop the board with your feet as you lift & turn off the ground.
  4. Prepare to land facing the opposite way & ride away!

Variatons of Spins

  • FRONTSIDE Spins
  • BACKSIDE Spins
Frontside
Turning your front shoulder outward as you spin.
Backside
Turning your front shoulder inward as you spin.
