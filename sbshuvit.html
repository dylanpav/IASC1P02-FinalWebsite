THE POP SHOVE-IT

also known as The Shuvit

The Pop Shove-it is simply a 180° rotation of the board, whilst the rider maintains his direction. Much like the Spin Ollie, the Shuvit can be performed as a Frontside or Backside.

What's The Difference?

Frontside
Pushing your back foot forward as you rotate the board.
Backside
Pulling your back foot backwards as you rotate the board.

3 Shove is a trick where the board rotates 360° when you shuvit.
