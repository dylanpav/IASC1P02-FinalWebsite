THE OLLIE

The foundation to a vast majority of tricks

How to execute:

  1. Jump foward using your back leg forcefully.
  2. Kick your front foot up and foward to stabalize the board.
  3. Push both feet towards the ground with the board (hopefully) beneath them.
  4. Ride away!

Nollie vs Ollie

Nollies are done the same way as Ollies however, the jump is performed on the front of the board.This means the roles of the front and back feet become reversed.
